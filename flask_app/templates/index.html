How to Use
  1. Enter your project name and video ID in the Settings panel, then fill in optional metadata.
  2. Click Define Region to draw a rectangle over the video player in your browser.
  3. Navigate to the target video in another browser tab and begin playback.
  4. Click Launch Control Panel — a floating window appears. Press Start (or Space) to begin capture.
  5. Press Stop when the video ends. The session saves automatically and the panel closes.
  6. Check Corpus Status below to review your accumulated data. Export to CSV when ready.
